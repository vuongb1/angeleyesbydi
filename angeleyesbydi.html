Appointment Policies

Good to know
before you book

Payment
Remaining balance must be paid in cash at the end of your appointment. A $20 deposit is required to book and is applied toward your total.
Cancellation
Cancel within 24 hours and your deposit will be refunded. Less than 24 hours notice — deposit is kept. Please be respectful of appointment times.
Late Policy
There is a 15-minute grace period. After 20 minutes your appointment may be cancelled and your deposit will be kept. Please plan accordingly!
No Show
After 2 no-shows, you will be banned from booking. Please communicate if you need to reschedule — it's always appreciated.
Come Makeup-Free
Please arrive makeup-free to your appointment. A $10 fee will be charged if you arrive with eye makeup on. This helps ensure the best lash retention.
Fill Requirements
Fills require at least 40% of lashes remaining. Less than 40% will be charged as a full set. Quick fills require 50–60% retention and must be within a week of your last service.
No Foreign Fills
I do not fill lashes done by another artist. A removal and full set will need to be booked instead.
Allergies
Any allergies or sensitivities must be communicated before the service. I will not be held responsible for reactions if not disclosed beforehand.
