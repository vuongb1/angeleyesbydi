Appointment Policies

Good to know
before you book

💜 Payment
Remaining balance must be paid in cash. A $20 deposit is required to book and applied to your total.
⏰ Cancellation
Cancel within 24 hours for a refund. Less than 24 hours notice — deposit is kept.
🕐 Late Policy
15-minute grace period. After 20 minutes your appointment may be cancelled and deposit kept.
🚫 No Show
After 2 no-shows, you will be banned from booking. Please communicate if you need to reschedule.
✨ Come Makeup-Free
Arrive makeup-free. A $10 fee will be charged if you arrive with eye makeup on.
👁️ Fill Requirements
Fills require at least 40% lashes remaining. Less than 40% will be charged as a full set.
🚫 No Foreign Fills
I do not fill lashes done by another artist. A removal and full set will be booked instead.
🌸 Allergies
All allergies and sensitivities must be shared before your service. I am not responsible for undisclosed reactions.
